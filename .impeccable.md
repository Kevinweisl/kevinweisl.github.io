# Design Context

## Target Audience
Academic peers, potential collaborators, graduate students, and the ccClub community (4,000+ learners). Visitors are primarily researchers in NLP/IR, faculty, and industry professionals exploring collaboration.

## Use Cases
- Learn about Kevin's research and publications
- Find contact information for collaboration
- Read Kevin's notes/articles on research and community building
- Understand Kevin's background (academia + 6 years industry)

## Brand Personality / Tone
- **Professional but approachable**: Academic rigor without being cold or intimidating
- **Knowledgeable but warm**: Bridges research and real-world applications
- **Distinctive but not flashy**: Memorable without being distracting from content

## Existing Brand Colors
- ccClub (Kevin's organization): Teal `#377A82` + Coral Red `#D24E42`
- Current site: Blue-purple gradient (flagged as AI-default by Impeccable audit)
- Kevin's instinct: prefers blue-purple family but open to change

## Constraints
- Must work in both light and dark mode
- Static export (GitHub Pages), Next.js + Tailwind CSS
- Serif headings (Young Serif) + Sans body (Albert Sans) with Noto Sans/Serif TC for CJK
